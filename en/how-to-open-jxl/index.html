Why won't my .jxl file open?

A .jxl file is a JPEG XL image — a modern format that makes files smaller at the same quality. It's perfectly valid; the problem is that most browsers, phones and apps simply haven't turned on support for it yet. Google removed JPEG XL from Chrome in 2022 and is bringing it back in 2026, so native support is improving — but until it's everywhere, the reliable fix is to view it with a JPEG XL viewer or convert it.
