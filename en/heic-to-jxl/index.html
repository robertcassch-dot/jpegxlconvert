JPEG XL offers HEIC-like compression but is an open standard, not tied to Apple — Safari already supports it and Chrome is rolling it out.
Can it stay lossless?
Yes — tick Lossless for perfect quality, or use the slider for smaller files.
Is it free and private?

Is JPEG XL as small as HEIC?
JPEG XL offers compression in the same class as HEIC, with the big advantage of being an open standard rather than Apple-only.

Tick Lossless for an exact copy; use the slider for smaller files when a tiny, invisible quality trade-off is fine.
