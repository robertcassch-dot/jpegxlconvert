Good to know

WebP was a useful step up from JPG and PNG, but JPEG XL goes further. Converting WebP to JPEG XL gives you better compression at the same quality, support for higher bit depths, and a format built as an open, royalty-free standard designed to outlast WebP. Both lossy and lossless WebP files are handled.

If you're maintaining an image library or a website, moving to JPEG XL is a sensible future-proofing step. As always, the conversion happens locally in your browser, so nothing is uploaded — you can batch-convert freely, with no limits and no watermark.
