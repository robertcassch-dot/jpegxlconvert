Privacy & Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

The short version

Your images and files are never uploaded. Every conversion runs entirely inside your own browser using WebAssembly. We never see, receive, store or transmit the files you convert.

Who we are

This website ("JXL Convert", jpegxlconvert.com) is operated by Robert Castillo. For any privacy question you can contact us at contact@jpegxlconvert.com.

What we do NOT collect

  • The images or files you convert — they stay on your device.
  • Account data — there is no sign-up and no login.
  • Names, addresses or payment data — the tool is free.

What we may collect

  • Anonymous usage statistics (e.g. page views, country, device type) via a privacy-friendly analytics tool, to understand how the site is used.
  • Advertising data: once advertising is enabled, third-party partners such as Google AdSense may use cookies to show ads. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites.

Cookies

A cookie is a small file stored by your browser. We may use cookies for analytics and advertising. You can disable or delete cookies in your browser settings at any time. Where required, we ask for your consent before non-essential cookies are set.

Google's use of advertising cookies is governed by Google's policies. You can opt out of personalised advertising via Google Ads Settings.

Your rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to access, correct, delete or restrict the processing of your personal data, and to object to processing or lodge a complaint with your data protection authority. To exercise any of these rights, contact contact@jpegxlconvert.com.

Changes

We may update this policy. The "last updated" date above reflects the latest version.
