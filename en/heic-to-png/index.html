Good to know

Converting HEIC to PNG gives you a lossless copy of your iPhone photo that opens in any editor or app that can't read HEIC. Because PNG keeps every pixel exactly, it's the right choice when you plan to edit the image, need maximum quality, or want to drop it into design and screenshot workflows.

As with all conversions here, your photos stay completely private — everything is processed on your device with WebAssembly and nothing is uploaded. If you only want to share the photo and care about file size, JPG will be smaller; choose PNG when fidelity comes first.
