Why won't my .jxl file open?

JPEG XL is a new image format. Most browsers, phones and apps haven't enabled it yet, so a downloaded .jxl often won't open — even though it's a perfectly valid image. This viewer decodes it locally so you can see it right away, and lets you save a universal copy as JPG or PNG. Need the file in another format permanently? Use the JXL to JPG converter or JXL to PNG converter.
